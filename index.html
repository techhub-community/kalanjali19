About Us

Susamskriti-The Cultural Forum of Sir MVIT, Bengaluru, has been celebrating Kalanjali, an annual intercollegiate Techno-Cultural extravaganza successfully since 1996. Kalanjali has been setting the stage for the student community to showcase their talents, innovation and creativity with zeal and zest. Kalanjali'18 is scheduled to be held on 12th and 13th of October 2018.

The objectives of the events are:

1.To enhance the inquisitive spirit
and knowledge horizon.
2.To bring out creative talents.
3.To generate competitive skills.
4.To nurture communication skills
and leadership qualities among
the students.
